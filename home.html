Since 2021, Delivering Fresh, Premium Fruits

Welcome to Rabal, your trusted partner for premium quality fruits, where we take pride in our carefully curated selection of apples, including the crisp and juicy Apple Gala, the sweet and tangy Kulu Delicious, the tart and crunchy Granny Smith, and the plump and flavorful Cherry.

About Us

At Rabal, we're passionate about fruits and dedicated to building long-term relationships with our customers. Our team is comprised of experienced professionals who share a common goal: to deliver the best fruits to every customer, every time.

We believe in the importance of sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction. That's why we work closely with local farmers and orchardists to source our fruits, ensuring that every purchase supports the local community.

Our fruits are sourced directly from local farmers and orchardists, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavor and nutrition.

Explore our website, rabal.in to learn more about our products, their benefits, and how we're committed to delivering excellence in every way.

Product Descriptions

Apple Gala

Description: Indulge in the crisp and juicy sweetness of our Apple Gala! With its vibrant red skin and creamy white flesh, this variety is perfect for snacking, salads, and baking.

  • High in antioxidants and fiber
  • May help reduce cholesterol levels and improve heart health
  • Supports healthy digestion and bowel function

Kulu Delicious

Description: Experience the sweet and tangy taste of our Kulu Delicious apples! With their bright red skin and crunchy texture, these apples are perfect for snacking, cooking, and making delicious desserts.

  • Rich in vitamins A and C, potassium, and fiber
  • May help boost immune function and reduce inflammation
  • Supports healthy weight management and satiety

Red Delicious Apple

Description: Known for its deep red color and mildly sweet flavor, the Red Delicious apple offers a crisp texture and is a classic favorite for fresh snacking.

  • High in antioxidants, particularly polyphenols, which may help prevent cancer and heart disease.
  • Rich in dietary fiber, supporting digestive health.
  • Contains vitamin C, contributing to a robust immune system.

Granny Smith

Description: Discover the tart and crunchy goodness of our Granny Smith apples! With their green skin and firm texture, these apples are perfect for baking, cooking, and making delicious pies and crisps.

  • High in antioxidants, fiber, and vitamins A and C
  • May help reduce cholesterol levels and improve heart health
  • Supports healthy digestion and bowel function

Golden Delicious Apple

Description: Golden Delicious apples are crisp and juicy with a sweet taste that has a bit of tartness to balance it out.

  • Rich in fiber, aiding digestive health by regulating bowel movements.
  • Contains quercetin, which may provide protection against cardiovascular disease and improve mental health.
  • Versatile in culinary uses, suitable for both fresh consumption and cooking.

Cherry

Description: Enjoy the sweet and plump goodness of our Cherries! With their vibrant red skin and juicy flesh, these fruits are perfect for snacking, baking, and making delicious desserts.

  • Rich in antioxidants, vitamins A and C, and potassium
  • May help reduce inflammation and improve heart health
  • Supports healthy sleep patterns and cognitive function

Plums

Description: Plums are sweet, juicy stone fruits with smooth skin, available in various colors and sizes. They offer a delightful balance of sweetness and tartness.

  • High in antioxidants, helping to prevent and manage osteoporosis, macular degeneration, diabetes, and obesity.
  • May improve cognition, boost immunity, and support nervous system health.
  • Aids in skin care and may help relieve indigestion and anxiety-related problems.
